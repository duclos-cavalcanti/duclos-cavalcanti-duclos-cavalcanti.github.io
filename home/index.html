Daniel Duclos-Cavalcanti

I’m a Computer Engineer living in New York with a bachelors and masters from the Technical University of Munich, in Germany. Currently, collaborating on research with Dr.Sivaraman and PhD. Haseeb Ashfaq from Systems@NYU.

The project revolves around Jasper: A Scalable Design and Implementation of a Financial Exchange in the Cloud. Beyond co-authoring and aiding in the development of Jasper, my thesis constructed a performant heuristic to select VMs based on present cloud conditions to improve Jasper’s overall performance, both before and during runtime.

  • Jasper: Design And Implementation of A Scalable Financial Exchange in the Cloud
  • SIGCOMM24 POSTER - Jasper, A Scalable and Fair Multicast for Fin. Exch. in the Cloud
  • Cloud TreeFinder: Private until October 3rd

Beyond my recent collaboration around cloud-based distributed systems research, I also have a background in Embedded Systems and FPGA Engineering, as well as developing on and for Linux/UNIX systems. Feel free to contact me or take a look at my resume.
